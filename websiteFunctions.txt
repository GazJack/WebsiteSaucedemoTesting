Functionality:

1. User management
Login with username and password.
Access to the home page after logging in.

2. Shopping cart
Adding items to the cart.
Updating the cart and correct display of the quantity of items.
Quick access to the cart from the home page.
Remove items from the cart.

3. Product catalog
List of products with names, prices and photos.
Sorting items by price, name (A–Z or Z–A).
Ability to view detailed product information.

4. Order process
Transition to the checkout page ("Checkout").
Entering user information (name, surname, zip code).
Ability to cancel the order ("Cancel" button).
Order confirmation and notification of successful completion.

5. Logout functionality
The user can log out by clicking the "Logout" button.
After logging out, the user is redirected to the login page.
